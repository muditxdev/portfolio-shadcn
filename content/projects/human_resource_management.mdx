A robust Human Resource Management System featuring a four-panel architecture designed for Super Admin, HR, Team Lead, and Employee roles. The system provides comprehensive tools for attendance tracking, employee management, task assignment, and leave management.

## Core Features

### Multi-Panel Architecture
- **Super Admin Panel**
  - System configuration and oversight
  - Company-wide policy management
  - Global analytics and reporting
  - Role and permission control

- **HR Panel**
  - Employee lifecycle management
  - Recruitment and onboarding
  - Performance evaluation
  - Compensation management
  - Training coordination

- **Team Lead Panel**
  - Team performance monitoring
  - Task distribution and tracking
  - Attendance verification
  - Leave approval workflow
  - Resource allocation

- **Employee Portal**
  - Personal profile management
  - Attendance and leave tracking
  - Task management
  - Document submission
  - Performance reviews

### Attendance Management
- **Time Tracking**
  - Automated check-in/out system
  - Break time monitoring
  - Overtime calculation
  - Shift management
  - Remote work tracking

### Employee Management
- **Profile Management**
  - Personal information
  - Employment history
  - Document repository
  - Skills and certifications
  - Emergency contacts

- **Performance Tracking**
  - KPI monitoring
  - Goal setting and tracking
  - Regular assessments
  - Feedback management
  - Recognition system

### Task Management
- **Project Coordination**
  - Task creation and assignment
  - Progress monitoring
  - Deadline tracking
  - Resource allocation
  - Performance metrics
